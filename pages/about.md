Behind the moniker Ryefield Society hides a French one-man project, delivering intimate electronic music composed of melancholic melodies, abstract textures and downtempo beats. Originally from France but now based in Zurich (Switzerland), Fantin started producing music at the age of 16, in 2010. After a decade spent exploring different musical approaches and genres - including electronics, post-rock and black metal -, he released his [first EP]({% link pages/releases/tapoa.md  %}) on Spleencore Records in 2020. After contributing to multiple compilations, he released his first LP entitled [Artless Ostinati]({% link pages/releases/artless-ostinati.md  %}) on label Huinali Recordings in 2023. The next year, he collaborated with the Portugal-based english artist James A. McDermid on a split album titled [S'accommoder/Perdido]({% link pages/releases/saccommoder-perdido.md  %}), released by ROHS! Recordings. More recently, he joined CAMP Radio, where he hosts his own show titled [Contemporary Feelings]({% link pages/mixes.html %}). In 2026, he released an introspective EP titled [Disturbance]({% link pages/releases/disturbance.md  %}), which takes a darker, more bass-driven turn compared to his previous releases.
